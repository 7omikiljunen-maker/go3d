How to play Go 3D

Goal

Score more points than your opponent by capturing their stones and surrounding territory — on a 3D cubic grid instead of a flat board.

Placing stones

Black goes first. Players take turns tapping an empty intersection to place a stone. Press Pass to skip your turn.

Liberties & captures

Each stone has up to 6 neighbours (left, right, up, down, forward, back). A stone or connected group is captured when all its neighbours are occupied by the opponent — it has no liberties left.

Ko rule

You cannot place a stone that returns the board to exactly its previous state. This prevents infinite loops.

Ending the game

The game ends when both players Pass consecutively. The player with the higher score wins.

Scoring

Captures — stones you have taken.
Territory — empty intersections completely surrounded by your colour.
Komi — White gets +6.5 bonus points to compensate for Black moving first.

Controls

Tap / click — place a stone.
Drag — rotate the board.
Scroll / pinch — zoom in and out.
Layer buttons (L1 – L11) — show or hide individual layers to see inside the cube.
Undo — take back the last move (both players must agree in online games).

Modes

PvP — two players on the same device.
PvC — you play Black, the computer plays White.
CvC — watch the computer play itself.
🌐 Online — play against a friend on another device.

AI Difficulty

In PvC and CvC modes you can choose how strong the computer plays:

Easy — plays randomly quite often, very beatable for beginners.
Medium — balanced default, thinks a few moves ahead.
Hard — searches deeper and picks the best move it finds, no randomness.

Change it any time in ⚙️ Settings → AI DIFFICULTY.

Settings ⚙️

Tap ⚙️ to change board size (3×3×3 up to 9×9×9), game mode, AI difficulty, scoring method, sound, and colour theme.

Install as an app

Tap ⬇ Install in ⚙️ Settings (or the install banner at the bottom of the screen) to add Go 3D to your home screen or desktop. Once installed, you can play single-player modes (PvP, PvC, CvC) offline — no internet needed. Online games still require a connection.

Playing online

Open ⚙️ Settings and tap 🌐 Play Online →.

To host a game: sign in with Google, then tap ✦ Create game. Creating a game requires a one-time €1 payment — paid once, valid forever. Share the 6-letter room code with your friend.

To join a game: enter the 6-letter code and tap Join game →. Joining is always free.

You play Black as host and White as guest. Moves sync instantly. Tap 💬 for in-game chat. Tap Leave to exit.
